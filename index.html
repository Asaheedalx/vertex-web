About Vertex Nextgen

Vertex Nextgen Solutions was founded by Saheed Adepoju, a Field Network Engineer with practical experience in telecom infrastructure and hybrid power systems.

The company focuses on helping businesses move from unstable daily operations to structured working systems. We combine legal setup, power stability, connectivity, and monitoring into a single support structure so businesses can operate with fewer interruptions.

Our Approach: Assess properly, install correctly, and leave the client with a system they understand and can depend on.
